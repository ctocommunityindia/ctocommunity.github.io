About Us

We are a community of CTOs and technology leaders collaborating to drive innovation and share knowledge.

Founding Member: Richa Kumari

Our Vision

To embrace technology as a catalyst for innovation, share knowledge to empower the community, learn from industry experts, and build meaningful connections with like-minded professionals. Through continuous learning and networking, we aim to drive personal and professional growth, fostering leadership opportunities and career advancement.

Our Mission

Grow a 20K-member community in one year through:

  • Structured Organization – Define roles & responsibilities
  • Open-Source Website – Central hub for engagement
  • Content & Knowledge Sharing – Blogs, podcasts, Q&A templates
  • Expert Engagement – Internal & external industry meetups
  • Community Events – Monthly calendar, planned conferences
  • Feedback & Collaboration – Bi-weekly meetings (2nd & 4th Saturday)
  • Promotion & Outreach – Expanding reach & visibility
